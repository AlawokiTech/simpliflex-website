The Problem

Most learning apps weren't built for our reality

Nigerian parents want their children learning — but the everyday apps work against them.

📶

They need data

Streaming lessons burns through expensive data, and stops the moment the network drops.

📺

They're full of ads

Free apps push adverts, pop-ups and in-app purchases at children all day long.

🎯

They're not structured

Random videos and games — nothing aligned to what a child should actually be learning.

🙈

Parents can't see progress

You hand over the phone and hope. No record of what was learned or practised.

Simpliflex Learn fixes all four — a structured, offline, ad-free world a child can explore safely, with a dashboard that shows you exactly what they're learning.
